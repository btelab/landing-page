ABOUT BTELab

The BTELab is a multidisciplinary team based at the Research Foundation of the General Hospital in Valencia, Spain. Created in 2020 and directed by Carmen Escobedo-Lucea, it is focused on research and development in the field of Biomedical and Tissue Engineering.
We seek to find new solutions to medical problems through the use of technologies within our areas of interest such as 3D printing and 3D bioprinting, organ-on-a-chip (OOC) or cell sheet, among others.
